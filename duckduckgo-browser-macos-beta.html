DuckDuckGo Browser MacOS Beta

Privacy is an often fleeting concept. Big tech keeps swooping in and introducing new ways to track users on their sites and beyond. However, DuckDuckGo is one company that is standing in the way of this constant surveillance expansion of big tech.

Highlights

Two-click data deletion
Easy to use
Modern UI
Email protection
Built-in tracker blocker
Duck Viewer

In my use of the browser, even in its beta state, I have found it to be a full-featured, powerhouse and bug free. While it is still in beta, I absolutely love this browser and cannot wait to see its prime-time release.

Read more about the browser on DuckDuckGo's blog, Spread Privacy.
